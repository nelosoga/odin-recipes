Easy Cottage Cheese Wrap

Description

This easy cottage cheese wrap is a keto-style flatbread, sprinkled with everything seasoning, a perfect base for a high-protein breakfast wrap.

The batter is whipped up in a blender, then poured on a baking sheet and baked.

Ingredients:

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup high protein cottage cheese, such as Good Culture®
  • ¼ teaspoon everything bagel seasoning

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 380 degrees F (193 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment and spray with nonstick spray.
  2. Add eggs and cottage cheese to the jar of a blender; blend on high until smooth, about 30 seconds. Pour mixture onto the prepared pan in a roughly rectangular shape. Sprinkle bagel seasoning over mixture.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Remove immediately. Allow to cool about 5 minutes before removing wrap from parchment.
