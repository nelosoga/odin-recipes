Cottage Cheese

Description

This easy cottage cheese wrap is a keto-style flatbread, sprinkled with everything seasoning, a perfect base for a high-protein breakfast wrap.

The batter is whipped up in a blender, then poured on a baking sheet and baked.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup high protein cottage cheese, such as Good Culture®
  • ¼ teaspoon everything bagel seasoning
